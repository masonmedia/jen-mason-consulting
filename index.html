Jen Mason Consulting

People. Possibility. Purpose.

Mission

Enabling groups to think, learn, and work, together

I am passionate about supporting learning and positive change in groups of all kinds. Blending over 20 years of experience as an educator and process designer with insights from my Ph.D. research about the role of listening in building resilience in complex, adaptive systems, I work with a sense of both purpose and playfulness. My goal is support effective communication and learning, which will enable your group to achieve its goals and thrive.

Systems Thinking

Healthy systems (groups, organizations, or communities) are those in which there is an open flow of information and ideas.

Conflict Patterns

That flow can be interrupted, or blocked by conflict, lack of clarity and trust, and habitual egoic listening patterns.

Blockage

When the pathways of communication become blocked, the system cannot function optimally in terms of either repair or growth.

Address and transform conflicts within your group to get unstuck

Conflict Transformation

Transformational team building is a process that enables and empowers groups to work through the issues and conflicts that limit them. Based on the work of master facilitator, Linda Lehtonen, this approach supports “focused, courageous, dialogue that helps the group move to action and resolution and take continuing responsibility for solving their issues on an ongoing basis” (Lehtonen).

About Jen

Enabling groups to Think, Learn, and Work, together

I have a Ph.D. in Sustainability Education and a Master’s degree in Education from Prescott College, and I have a Bachelor of Education degree with a focus in Outdoor and Experiential Education and a Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science from Queen’s University. My work and research sit at the confluence of the following: sustainability education, resilience and complexity theory, multi-stakeholder whole-system change processes, dialogue theory and practice, organizational learning, mindfulness, and transformative learning theory. Bringing my love of learning and deep humility, I work with a sense of thoughtful purpose and infectious playfulness. I’d love to connect with you to find out how I can support you and your team/organization/community.
